JournAI

JournAI is an AI-powered online journaling platform developed using HTML, CSS, and Python, integrating a RAG model to enhance user interaction and memory recall. Acting as a digital second brain, it allows users to record their experiences and intelligently retrieve past entries or specific contact-related information. By leveraging the RAG model, JournAI combines document retrieval with generative AI to provide accurate, context-aware responses, making memory search more intuitive and meaningful. The platform is designed to reduce cognitive load by delivering structured, AI-assisted insights that help users reflect, remember, and organize their thoughts more effectively.
